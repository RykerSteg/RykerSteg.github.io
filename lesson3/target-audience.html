Target Audience

Audience

This site is open to all individuals and families but caters primarily to the residents and visitors of Southern Utah. Whether you are planning a vacation to Zion National Park or just want to know what weather and events are coming up in your area, this site is for you.

Darcy Wolverdeen

Meet Darcy! Darcy is an outdoor enthusiast who loves to travel. She's not from around these parts but she vacations out to Zion National Park often. She is constantly checking her smart phone to see if it is going to be a good time for a getaway. She also likes to check in and see what events are taking place in the area. She may not be in the area all the time but she likes to know when he can visit her favorite vacation spot.

Nick stone

Meet Nick! Nick is a Park Maintenance Operator for local municipality. His job requires him to work ourdoors and he likes to plan his projects ahead of time so knowing the forecast is essential. Whether it is preparing for the extreme heat or just knowing when the rain is coming, Nick needs to know the weather. For his job he also needs to be aware of local events that may affect his parks and grounds. He usually accesses the site through his smartphone but occasionally uses a laptop.

Scenarios

  • What's the weather like this week?
  • When is the next major local event?
  • Is the weather good enough to go hiking in Zion National Park?
  • When is prime fishing season for Sand Hollow and Quail lake?
  • What is going on this 4th of July in Southern Utah?
